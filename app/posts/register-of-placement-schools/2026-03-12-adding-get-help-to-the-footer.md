Users who need help or have a question need a way to contact the team. Without visible contact information, users may not know how to get support or where to find it.

We added a 'Get help' section to the footer so users can find support contact information on every page.

## Why we did this

Contact information was not consistently available across the service. Users who needed support had no obvious place to look.

Placing help information in the footer makes it available on every page without adding it to the main content of individual pages.

## How it works

The 'Get help' section appears in the footer, below the navigation links.

It includes:

- an email address: <becomingateacher@digital.education.gov.uk>
- a response time message: 'You'll get a response within 5 working days'

The response time message sets clear expectations about when users can expect a reply. This reduces uncertainty for users waiting for a response.
